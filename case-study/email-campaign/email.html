Aegis Facility & Security: B2B Lead-Nurture Email Funnel

A full-funnel email marketing project designed to guide new prospects from awareness to consultation through a structured, behavior-driven automation flow. The campaign focused on lifecycle strategy, responsive MJML development, and conversion-focused UX decisions.

Project Overview

This project focused on building a complete B2B lead-nurture funnel for a fictional security and facility solutions company. Instead of designing isolated emails, the goal was to create a connected lifecycle system that guides users through awareness, education, trust-building, and conversion.

I mapped the funnel structure and conditional automation logic to determine how leads would progress based on engagement behavior. Each email was intentionally designed to support a different stage of the journey — from educational insights to solution positioning and social proof.

Emails were developed using MJML with responsive layouts, accessibility considerations, and strategic CTA placement to improve clarity and engagement across devices. The project emphasized real-world email UX principles such as content hierarchy, trust reinforcement, and low-friction conversion paths.

Beyond design, this case study highlights the strategic thinking behind lifecycle marketing — combining funnel psychology, automation planning, and technical email development into a cohesive system.

Key Highlights

  • Full Funnel Strategy

    Designed a 5-email lifecycle sequence aligned with awareness, education, solution, proof, and conversion stages.

  • Automation & Conditional Flow Planning

    Structured behavioral logic to adapt the journey based on engagement signals and CTA interaction.

  • Responsive MJML Development

    Built modular email layouts optimized for accessibility, readability, and cross-device performance.

  • Conversion-Focused UX

    Applied progressive CTA placement, trust sections, and review-driven design to support decision-making.

Email Funnel Examples

View Awareness Stage

Email 1 — Welcome & Authority

Introduced the brand and established trust using a structured welcome layout. The email focused on clarity, authority messaging, and early CTA placement without overwhelming the reader.

  • • Hero CTA placed early for high-intent users
  • • Authority-driven content blocks to build trust
  • • Low-pressure closing section to maintain nurture flow
 View Education Stage

Email 2 — Educational Insights

Focused on delivering value before promotion by highlighting common security risks. Educational positioning increased credibility while preparing users for solution-focused messaging later in the funnel.

  • • Educational risk cards to drive engagement
  • • Mid-body CTA aligned with informational intent
  • • Structured hierarchy for scan-friendly reading
 View Solution Stage

Email 3 — Integrated Solution Spotlight

Transitioned from education into solution positioning by introducing integrated monitoring systems. Messaging focused on outcomes rather than features to support decision-making.

  • • Conversion-focused CTA introduced gradually
  • • Benefits framed around operational efficiency
  • • Visual hierarchy guiding readers toward action
 View Social Proof Stage

Email 4 — Client Review & Trust Reinforcement

Strengthened credibility using testimonial-driven content designed to reduce hesitation. Social proof reinforced previous solution messaging and supported conversion readiness.

  • • Review-focused visual section for authenticity
  • • CTA positioned after trust-building content
  • • Minimal copy to maintain clarity and impact
 View Conversion Stage

Email 5 — Final Consultation Invite

Delivered a clear and confident next step while maintaining a supportive tone. FAQ-style content reduced friction and addressed common concerns before the final CTA.

  • • FAQ structure to lower decision anxiety
  • • Strong but non-aggressive conversion CTA
  • • Reinforces full funnel journey from prior emails
